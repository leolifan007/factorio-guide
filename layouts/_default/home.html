About Factorio

Automate Everything. Launch to Space.

Factorio is a factory-building simulation game by Wube Software. Players design and manage production chains across mining, smelting, manufacturing, and research — all while defending against hostile alien biters.

The game offers two major expansion campaigns: Dyson Sphere Program (not related to this site) and the official Space Age expansion, which adds five unique planets with different resources and mechanics.

This guide covers both the base game and Space Age expansion, from first-hour survival to launching a megabase capable of 1,000+ SPM (science per minute).
