## 开篇第一句模板（随机选用，不做固定 rotation）
强调：必须直接甩痛点和数字，不准有 "In this guide"、"Whether you're a" 类开场

模板池：
- "Your [problem] is not [X]. It's [Y]."
- "If your [thing] is [broken], stop looking at [obvious cause]. It's [real cause]."
- "You don't need [complex solution]. You need [simple thing]."
- "Here's the number most guides get wrong: [number]."
- "[Problem] — here's the fix in one sentence."
- "I've seen [X] factory tours stall at this exact point. Here's why."
- "The official wiki tells you [thing]. What it doesn't tell you: [real thing]."
