## Automation & First Science Packs

### How to Automate Iron Plates
The first automated product most factories make. Set up a simple smelting array and belt it back to your assemblers.

### How to Make Red Science
Red Science Pack = 1 Copper Cable + 1 Iron Gear Wheel. This is your first automated research milestone.

### How to Make Green Science
Green Science Pack = 1 Electronic Circuit. The gate to the mid-game — requires circuits, which require more copper.

### How to Unlock Automation
Automation 1 technology unlocks assemblers. Build your first inserter-fed assembler and you're in business.

## Key Beginner Mistakes

1. **Building too small** — Overestimate what you need. 4-lane bus beats 2-lane. Rebuilding is painful.
2. **Power bottlenecks** — Add boilers before you run out of power. Nuclear is easier than it looks.
3. **Spaghetti bases** — When green science gets cramped, tear down and rebuild with a proper bus.
4. **No belt balancers** — Splitters distribute evenly. A single inserter to 3 machines will starve the last.
5. **Ignoring biters** — Wall + gun turret + ammo is the minimum defense. Don't skip it.
