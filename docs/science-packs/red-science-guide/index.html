Red Science Pack — Automation Science Guide

Everything about the first science pack in Factorio: exact recipe, assembler count, and how to automate it properly.

Recipe

1xIron gear
+
1xIron plate
→
1xAutomation science

Assembler Math

Production Goal Assemblers Needed Notes
1 SP (science pack) per second 1 assembler Slow, hand-fed
5 SPM (sustainable) 5 assemblers Needs iron plate belt
10 SPM 10 assemblers + 1 gear assembler Typical early game

Tip: Red science is cheap. Don't overthink it. 10 assemblers producing red science is more than enough for the entire early game research queue.

How to Automate

  1. Belt iron plates into the assembler
  2. Belt iron gears into the assembler (or hand-craft a few stacks)
  3. Use an inserter to pull completed science packs into a chest or directly into lab input
Common mistake: New players often forget that labs need both science packs AND power. Check that your lab has an inserter feeding it!

Research Priority After Red Science

  1. Steel processing — unlocks steel plates
  2. Engine — unlocks engine units (needed for blue science)
  3. Automation 2 — unlocks electric mining drill
  4. Logistics — unlocks green science (electronic circuit + iron gear)
